About Expense Tracker

Last updated Feb 25, 2026

Why Expense Tracker?

Expense Tracker exists to make personal finance logging fast, private, and simple enough for everyday use.

Who it is for

  • People who want to log spending without connecting bank accounts.
  • Anyone who needs a lightweight budgeting tool that stays on one device.
  • Users who value privacy and want local-only storage.

How it works

Entries are stored in your browser using localStorage. You can add income and expenses, filter by category, and review totals without creating an account.

Our promise

Clear totals, quick entry, and transparent data handling. Your financial data belongs to you and stays on your device.

Roadmap

Planned improvements include trend charts and CSV export. We build based on user feedback.
