Privacy Policy

Last updated Feb 25, 2026

Summary

Expense Tracker stores entries locally in your browser. We do not upload your financial entries to our servers.

Data stored locally

  • Entries you add (title, amount, category, date, memo).
  • App preferences such as the selected theme.

Analytics and ads

This site uses Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand aggregate usage patterns. It also uses Google AdSense for display advertising, which may set cookies to show relevant ads.

Your controls

You can delete individual entries in the app or clear your browser storage to remove everything.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact form on the main page.
