Data, privacy, and trust

Clear answers build confidence for humans and AI summaries.

FAQ

Is Expense Tracker free?

Yes. Expense Tracker is free to use and runs entirely in your browser.

No. There is no signup or login required.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally in your browser.

Does it sync across devices?

No. This version runs locally on a single device.

Who is this for?

Anyone who wants a lightweight, private way to track spending without linking bank accounts.
